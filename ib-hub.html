About This Course

IB Sports, Exercise & Health Science

📐 Specification Structure

IB SEHS is organised around three major interconnected themes, explored at both Standard Level (SL) and Higher Level (HL). The course emphasises scientific inquiry, data analysis and the application of theory to real sporting contexts.

  • Theme 1 — Exercise Physiology and Nutrition of the Human Body
  • Theme 2 — Biomechanics
  • Theme 3 — Sport Psychology and Motor Learning
  • Internal Assessment — Scientific Investigation (20% of final grade)
  • HL Extension — Additional depth in all three themes

What Is the IB SEHS Internal Assessment?

The IA is an individual scientific investigation worth 20% of your final grade at both SL and HL. You design a study, collect and process data, then write a structured report evaluated against IB criteria.

Formulating a focused, measurable research question
Designing an ethical and valid methodology
Collecting, recording and processing quantitative data
Presenting data with appropriate graphs and statistical analysis
Evaluating methodology, identifying limitations and suggesting improvements

IB Command Terms

Know Exactly What Every Question Is Asking

IB examiners use specific command terms to define the depth and type of answer required. Understanding these is essential for exam success.

Define
Give the precise meaning of a word, phrase or concept.
Describe
Give a detailed account of facts, characteristics or events without explanation.
Explain
Give a detailed account with reasons or causes. Show how or why something occurs.
Analyse
Break down information into its component parts and examine each part in detail.
Evaluate
Make an appraisal by weighing up strengths and limitations. Come to a conclusion.
Justify
Give valid reasons or evidence to support an answer or conclusion.
Discuss
Offer a considered and balanced review including a range of arguments and evidence.
Compare
Give an account of both similarities and differences between two or more items.
Outline
Give a brief account or summary of key points only.
Apply
Use knowledge and understanding in a response that relates to a specific situation.
Design
Produce a plan, simulation or model. Used particularly in IA methodology.
Process
Carry out or describe the steps taken to obtain a result. Used in IA data processing.
