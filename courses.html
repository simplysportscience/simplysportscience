Need a Hand Choosing?

Not Sure Which Course Is Yours?

If you're not sure which hub matches your qualification, here's a quick way to check

Studying the IB Diploma?

If your school follows the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme, you're studying IB Sports, Exercise & Health Science (SEHS).

Studying Cambridge International?

If your school follows Cambridge International qualifications, you're studying AS Level Sport & Physical Education (8386).

Doing a vocational, unit-based course?

If your qualification is built from units and assignments rather than a single final exam, you're studying BTEC Sport (Pearson International Level 3).

Studying UK GCSE PE?

The GCSE PE hub is coming soon. In the meantime, the A–Z Library and Student Hub cover plenty of shared foundation content.
