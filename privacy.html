🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Note: This is a starting template covering the basics of what Simply Sport Science collects and why. Before relying on it, it's worth having it reviewed by a legal professional — particularly around UK GDPR and data protection for users under 18, since many students using this site are minors.

Who We Are

Simply Sport Science (simplysportscience.co.uk) is an educational platform created by Phil Mathe, providing sport science learning resources for IB SEHS, Cambridge AS Level Sport & PE, BTEC Sport and GCSE PE students and teachers. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at hello@simplysportscience.co.uk.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact form submissions — if you use the contact form on our About page, we collect your name, email address and message.
  • Newsletter sign-up — if you subscribe for updates, we collect your email address.
  • Usage data — like most websites, our hosting and analytics tools may automatically collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type and pages visited, to help us understand how the site is used.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to messages sent through the contact form
  • Send newsletter updates to subscribers, when requested
  • Understand how the site is used, so we can improve it

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Cookies & Third-Party Content

Some pages on this site embed videos from YouTube. When you view an embedded video, YouTube (Google) may set its own cookies and collect data according to its own privacy policy. We don't control this directly — you can find out more from Google's privacy policy.

Data Retention

We keep contact form and newsletter information only for as long as it's needed for the purpose it was collected — for example, until your enquiry is resolved, or until you unsubscribe from the newsletter.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Withdraw consent (for example, unsubscribe from the newsletter) at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@simplysportscience.co.uk.

Children's Privacy

Simply Sport Science is designed to be used by school students, many of whom are under 18. We only collect the minimum information necessary (such as an email address for the newsletter, or contact form details), and we encourage students to check with a parent, guardian or teacher before submitting any personal information through this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@simplysportscience.co.uk.
