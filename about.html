The Story

Why I Built Simply Sport Science

My journey into teaching wasn't a straight line. I started my career in sales — but it didn't take long to realise that what I really wanted to do was work in physical education. A conversation with my wife was the turning point. I retrained, qualified as a PE teacher, and never looked back.

Even during my time in sales, I stayed connected to the world of sport and physical activity through youth sports coaching. That experience — working with young people outside of a school setting — gave me an early understanding of how much sport and movement matter beyond the curriculum. It shaped the philosophy that runs through everything I do: PE is about far more than sport. It is about movement, physical literacy, wellbeing and holistic development.

Over 20 years of teaching PE across UK and international schools, I have had the privilege of working with students and teachers across a wide range of courses and qualification pathways — from GCSE PE through to IB Sports, Exercise and Health Science. Teaching IB SEHS in an international school context was where the need for Simply Sport Science became most apparent. The syllabus is vast, complex and genuinely demanding — and the freely available resources online were nowhere near good enough.

"I built the resource I wished I had when I first started teaching sport science — something that explains the content clearly, uses the right specification language, and helps students understand not just what to learn but how to apply it in their exam."

Simply Sport Science started as a collection of videos and resources shared with my own students. When teachers from other schools began asking where they could access the same materials, I realised there was a genuine need for a proper platform — one that was specification-accurate, professionally presented and genuinely useful for both students and the teachers who recommend resources to them.

My research interest in retrieval practice — developed during my PGCE Top-up at the University of Northampton and continuing into my current PhD studies — has shaped how Simply Sport Science is designed. Every lesson, every practice check and every A–Z definition is built around the science of how people actually learn and retain information. Not just what to teach, but how to make it stick.

Alongside Simply Sport Science, my books Happiness Factories and Changemakers explore the broader philosophy of physical education — advocating for holistic, movement-centred approaches that put pupil development and wellbeing at the heart of what we do as PE educators.

If you are a student who needs clear, specification-aligned content — or a teacher who wants a resource they can trust and recommend — Simply Sport Science is here for you.

Our Mission

Sport Science Sorted — For Every Student, Everywhere.

Simply Sport Science exists to make high-quality, specification-aligned sport science education accessible to every student and teacher — wherever they are in the world. Whether you are studying IB SEHS in an international school, preparing for Cambridge AS Level PE, working through BTEC Sport or revising GCSE PE — this platform is built for you.
