Getting Started

How to Use Simply Sport Science in Your Classroom

Whether you use the presentations as your main teaching resource or alongside your own materials, here is the quickest way to get started.

1

Choose Your Course

Select your course hub — IB Sport Science, Cambridge AS Level, BTEC Sport or GCSE PE — and browse the available lesson presentations.

2

Open in Canva or PowerPoint

Click the Canva link to open the presentation in your Canva account (free to use), or download the PowerPoint version directly.

3

Adapt if Needed

Add your school logo, adjust the content for your class level, or use it exactly as it is. All presentations are fully editable in Canva.

4

Share With Students

Share the PDF version with students as a lesson handout, or direct them to the corresponding lesson page on Simply Sport Science.

5

Use the Practice Checks

After the lesson, direct students to the Simply Sport Science practice check for that topic — built-in retrieval practice with instant feedback.

6

Explore the A–Z

Use the Simply Sport Science A–Z Library as a class reference — students can look up any key term with definitions and course tags.

Using Canva

Don't have Canva? Here's how to get started.

Canva is a free online design platform. You can create a free account at canva.com. Once you have an account, clicking any Canva link from Simply Sport Science will copy the presentation directly into your account, ready to edit and present.

✓ Free to use — no subscription required for basic editing
✓ Works in any browser on any device — no software to install
✓ Export as PowerPoint or PDF directly from Canva
✓ Share with students via a link — no account needed to view
